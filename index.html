Royal Ascot
Tipping Competition

Welcome to Hot to Trot’s Royal Ascot tipping competition, where our members test their tipping skills and battle it to become our tipping champion!

It’s easy to get involved, all you need to do is select one horse in each race (to win or each-way) and try to accrue the most profit throughout the festival.

MORE DETAILS:

Results are calculated with the results on SportingLife, using the odds at SP.

NR selections will be replaced with £10 to win on the favourite. Place terms: under 5 runners (win only), 5-7 (2), 8-12 (3), 13-20 (4) and 21+ (5).
